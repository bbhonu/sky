February

  • 10

    Penumbral Lunar Eclipse

    A penumbral eclipse is when Earth passes between the moon and sun. This obscures the Sun’s light and a shadow appears on the moon’s surface.

    This is a subtle event but is best visible after moonrise and before complete sunset.

  • 26

    Annular Solar Eclipse

    This eclipse will show the moon passing in front of the sun, creating a ring of light around the darkness of the moon.

    Will only be visible in South and West Africa and most of South America.
